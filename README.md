# Privacy Policy for Lumi Qube

**Effective Date:** January 30, 2026

At **Lumi Qube**, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

### 1. No Personal Data Collection
Lumi Qube does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share your name to play the game.

### 2. Local Data Storage
The only data stored by the application are your game statistics, such as High Scores and cleared lines. This information is stored **locally on your device**. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by the developer or any third party.

### 3. Permissions
The app may require standard system haptics/vibration for feedback, but it does not access your private data, location, or files.

### 4. Children's Privacy
Lumi Qube is safe for all ages and does not collect any data from children.

### 5. Third-Party Services
Lumi Qube does not use any third-party analytics or advertising networks that track user behavior.
