# Privacy Policy for Lumi Qube

**Effective Date:** January 30, 2026

At **Lumi Qube**, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

### 1. No Personal Data Collection
Lumi Qube does **not** directly collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share your name to play the game.

### 2. Local Data Storage
The only data stored by the application are your game statistics, such as High Scores and cleared lines. This information is stored **locally on your device**. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by the developer.

### 3. Third-Party Services
Lumi Qube uses the following third-party services:

**a) Google AdMob (Advertising)**  
We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to show personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:
- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- General location (country/region)
- App usage data

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

**b) Firebase (Remote Configuration)**  
We use Firebase Remote Config to manage app settings remotely. Firebase may collect:
- App instance identifiers
- Basic device information

For more information, see [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

### 4. Advertising & Tracking (iOS)
On iOS devices, the app requests your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before collecting data for personalized advertising. You can choose to allow or deny this request.

### 5. Permissions
The app may require standard system haptics/vibration for feedback. For advertising purposes, it may request tracking permission on iOS.

### 6. Children's Privacy
Lumi Qube is designed to be safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Advertisements shown are configured to be appropriate for general audiences.
